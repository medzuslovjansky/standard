# InterSlavic Linguistic Forum Foundation (MSJF)

The Foundation is an independent organization founded by Michał Swat. It is **not** the governing body of the Interslavic language — that role belongs to the [Committee](committee). Michał is a Committee member, but the Foundation is his separate project focused on education, cultural events, and linguistic research.

## Mission

The Foundation focuses on:
- Comparative linguistics research across Slavic languages
- Educational programs and language courses
- Cultural events and intercultural dialogue
- Interslavic language promotion and services
