# Events & Gatherings

Most events below were organized by or through the InterSlavic Linguistic Forum Foundation (MSJF), not the Committee itself. Poznań is the Foundation's base and primary event hub.

## 2024

### Second Interslavic Meeting — Poznań (March 17, 2024)

Preparatory gathering for linguistic training. First face-to-face conversations in Interslavic.

### Warsaw University Podcast (July 15, 2024)

Radio Kampus 97.1 FM science podcast. Discussion conducted entirely in Interslavic.

### Interslavic Gathering — Wrocław (August 2024)

Multi-country participants from Czechia, Poland, and Ukraine.

### Meeting with Jan van Steenbergen — Poznań (August 25, 2024)

14 attendees (half actively speaking Interslavic) from Czechia, Poland, Ukraine, and Netherlands. Grammatical discussions.

## 2025

### Intercultural Integration: Interslavic Singing — Poznań (May 12, 2025)

Pireus cultural center. Participants from Poland, Czech Republic, Belarus, Ukraine. Focused on immigrant/refugee integration in EU.

### Academic Presentation — Adam Mickiewicz University, Poznań (May 2025)

Presentations on Interslavic language and literature. Concert at Pan Gar restaurant. Organized by Nina Mautner.

### SLAVNI Festival (May 2025)

Booth with Interslavic merchandise. Glagolitic and Cyrillic script demonstrations. Literary analysis of "Nehaj nesųt nas běle oblåky" by Association for Interslavic Culture (Croatia).

### Interslavic Language Resolution Published (May 2025)

Committee document (not Foundation). Signed by five Committee members.

### Czech Television Reportage (April 13, 2025)

"Gejzír" on Česká Televize 1. Organized with Dr. Vojtěch Merunka.

### Musical Concert — Poznań (August 22, 2025)

Przystanek Pireus. Roberto Lombino performed. Sorbian guests from Lusatia (Dr. Filip Peršin, Sofia Brězanec, Daniela Heelemanowa). Funded by CIM Horyzonty and City of Poznań.

### Interslavic in America (September 2025)

Partnership development with Slavicbros (Slavic diaspora in America).
