# Appendix 4: Verbal Prefix Inventory

Prefixes attach primarily to verbs but may also modify nouns and adjectives. The base form of any prefixed verb is perfective; secondary imperfectives are derived from it.

---

## Complete Prefix List

| Prefix | Complementary | Primary spatial/directional sense | Aspectual/modifying function | Representative examples |
|--------|--------------|----------------------------------|------------------------------|------------------------|
| :isv[do-] | :isv[od-] | to, up to (the goal/end) | completion up to a limit; doing additionally | :isv[dojdti] "arrive", :isv[doletěti] "arrive by flight", :isv[dozvati] "call sb. to come"; :isv[dopisati] "add in writing", :isv[dopolniti] "fill by adding" |
| :isv[iz-] | :isv[v-] / :isv[vy-] | out, off, away from inside | ex-, out of | :isv[izgnati] "exile", :isv[izbrati] "pick out, select", :isv[izdati] "give out" |
| :isv[na-] | — | up, onto, over | perfective marker; doing a lot; (+ se) doing enough | :isv[navrgnuti] "throw onto", :isv[naložiti] "lay on", :isv[najdti] "find"; :isv[napisati] "write down"; :isv[nagovoriti] "speak a lot", :isv[najesti se] "eat one's fill" |
| :isv[nad-] | :isv[pod-] | over, above (motion) | surpassing, outdoing | :isv[nadsiliti] "overbear", :isv[nadigrati] "defeat, outplay", :isv[nadpisati] "write over", :isv[nadkryti] "cover over" |
| :isv[nedo-] | :isv[do-] | incompleteness | doing partially / not to the end | :isv[nedopisati] "write without finishing", :isv[nedopolniti] "fill only partially" |

| :isv[prěd-] | :isv[za-] | before, in front | doing in advance, beforehand | :isv[prědviděti] "foresee", :isv[prědstaviti] "introduce, imagine", :isv[prědplatiti] "pay in advance" |

| :isv[pro-] | — | through, past | thoroughness throughout; missing/going past; losing all | :isv[probiti] "break through", :isv[protěkti] "flow through", :isv[progrějati] "heat through"; :isv[projehati mimo] "drive past"; :isv[protratiti] "lose all (money)" |

| :isv[u-] | — | away | flying/running away; total (often negative) completion; making more (from adj.) | :isv[uletěti] "fly away", :isv[uběgti] "run away"; :isv[ubiti] "kill", :isv[uginuti] "die off", :isv[usohnuti] "dry up"; :isv[ulěpšiti] "improve", :isv[umenšiti] "reduce" |
| :isv[v-/vo-] | :isv[vy-] / :isv[iz-] | in, into (motion) | entering; (+ se) doing carefully/attentively | :isv[vojdti] "enter", :isv[vnesti] "bring in"; :isv[včitati se] "start reading closely", :isv[vslušati se] "start listening attentively" |

## Secondary Imperfective Formation from Prefixed Verbs

| Perfective ending | Imperfective suffix | Example |
|------------------|--------------------|---------||
| **-ati** | **-yvati** (hard) / **-ivati** (soft) | :isv[sodržati] → :isv[sodrživati] |
| **-iti** | **-jati** (vowel of stem lengthened) | :isv[obnoviti] → :isv[obnavjati] |
| **-nuti** | **-ati** | :isv[oddohnuti] → :isv[oddyhati] |
| monosyllabic + vowel | **-vati** | :isv[prodati] → :isv[prodavati] |

## Notes on Prefix Stacking and Interaction

- A single verb may carry two prefixes (e.g., :isv[prě-] + :isv[u-], :isv[nad-] + :isv[do-]), though this is uncommon.
- :isv[obez-] is a fused prefix (:isv[ob-] + :isv[bez-]) and always derives verbs meaning deprivation.
- :isv[nedo-] is a fused prefix (:isv[ne-] + :isv[do-]) expressing incomplete or insufficient action.
- Prefix :isv[ne-] on adjectives/nouns is strictly negative (not a verbal prefix): :isv[nedobry] "not good".
- Prefix :isv[naj-] on adjectives forms superlatives: :isv[najdobry] "best" (not a verbal prefix).
- South Slavic varieties use :isv[iz-] where North/West Slavic uses :isv[vy-]: both are valid in Interslavic.
