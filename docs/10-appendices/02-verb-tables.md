## Participles

:::caution[Unverified Content]
The past active participle example for nesti shows "ukradeši" but the source gives "ukradši" (from ukrasti < ukrad-ti). The form "ukradeši" with an inserted -e- does not match the source rule of adding -ši to consonant stems.
:::

| Participle | Formation | Example (dělati) | Example (nesti) | Example (hvaliti) |
|------------|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|------------------|
| Pres. active | 3pl pres. **-t → -či** | :isv[dělajuči] | :isv[nesuči] | :isv[hvaleči] |
| Past active | inf. stem **+ -vši** (vowel) / **-ši** (cons.) | :isv[sdělavši] | :isv[ukradeši] | — |
| Pres. passive | pres. stem **+ -omy/-emy** (I) / **-imy** (II) | :isv[dělajemy] | :isv[nesomy] | :isv[hvalimy] |
| Past passive | inf. stem **+ -ny** (vowel) / **-eny** (cons.) | :isv[dělany] | :isv[neseny] | :isv[hvaljeny] |
| L-participle | inf. stem **+ -l/-la/-lo/-li** | :isv[dělal/a/o/i] | :isv[nesl/a/o/i] | :isv[hvalil/a/o/i] |

**Special past passive rules:**
- **-iti** verbs: **-jeny** (not -iny): :isv[ljubiti]→:isv[ljubjeny], :isv[prositi]→:isv[prošeny]
- **-nuti** verbs: **-neny**: :isv[tegnuti]→:isv[tegneny]
- Monosyllabic stems (except **-a**): **-ty**: :isv[biti]→:isv[bity], :isv[čuti]→:isv[čuty]

### Verbal nouns (gerunds)

Replace **-ny → -nje** or **-ty → -tje**:
:isv[dělanje] "doing", :isv[nesenje] "carrying", :isv[pitje] "drinking"

## Verbs of Motion (three-way aspect)

:::caution[Unverified Content]
The source discusses the three-way aspect pattern for verbs of motion but only gives hoditi/idti/pojdti and jezditi/jehati as explicit examples. The full table with letati/letěti, nositi/nesti, voziti/vezti, běgati/běgti, and plovati/pluti is not present in the source material and appears to be extrapolated.

| Non-directional impf. | Directional impf. | Perfective |
|----------------------|------------------|-----------|

## Aspect Pair Formation

| Perfective ending | Imperfective derived by | Example |
|------------------|------------------------|---------|
| **-ati** | **→ -yvati** (hard) / **-ivati** (soft) | :isv[dokazati] → :isv[dokazyvati] |
| **-iti** | **→ -jati** (vowel lengthened) | :isv[napraviti] → :isv[napravjati] |

| monosyllabic stem | **→ -vati** | :isv[priznati] → :isv[priznavati] |
