# About This Specification

The **Interslavic Language Standard Specification** represents the collective effort of linguists, language enthusiasts, and cultural advocates dedicated to fostering unity among Slavic peoples through a common linguistic medium. It is built upon a foundation of shared heritage, mutual respect, and a vision for greater collaboration.

### Version Information

- **Current Version**: 1.0
- **Date of Publication**: October 1, 2023

### Revision History

- **Version 0.9**: Initial draft released for community feedback.
- **Version 1.0**: Incorporates revisions based on contributions and suggestions.

### Future Updates

The specification will undergo periodic reviews to incorporate advancements in linguistic research, address emerging needs, and refine existing guidelines.

> **Contact Information**  
> For feedback, suggestions, or inquiries, please contact the Interslavic Linguistic Committee at [contact@interslavic.org](mailto:contact@interslavic.org).

### Closing Remarks

Thank you for engaging with the **Interslavic Language Standard Specification**. Your interest and participation are vital to the success of this initiative. Together, we can preserve and celebrate the rich linguistic tapestry of the Slavic world.
