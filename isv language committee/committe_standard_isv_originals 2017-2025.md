# DECISIONS FROM 2018-07

## 1. Writing ŕ

**Scientific orthography:**
å ć ď đ ě ě ľ ń ò ŕ ś š ť ų ž

męsaŕ → męsaŕa → męsaŕami...


**Orthography without ŕ:**

męsar → męsara → męsarami...



## 2. Cyrillic and Latin script — rule: one Cyrillic letter = one Latin letter

- Å / Ӑ → (kråva / крӑва)  
- Ć / Ћ → (hoćų / хоћѫ)  
- Ď / ДЬ → (měď / мѣдь)  
- Đ / Ђ → (među / међу) *(better to write DŽ even in scientific system)* ⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️  
- Ę / Ѧ → (telę / телѧ)  
- Ľ / ЛЬ → only before J (e.g. *usiľje / усильје*), otherwise lj / ль – in Cyryllic there's no difference besides that non-scientific writing is „усилје”  
- Ń / НЬ → same as Ľ (e.g. *pytańje / пытаньје*), non-scientific пытанје, but *konj / конь*  
- Ò / Ъ → (kògda / къгда)  
- Ŕ / РЬ → (caŕ / царь, tvŕdy / ?твьрды? / ?тврьды?)  ⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️
- Ś / СЬ → (dneś / днесь)  
- Ť / ТЬ → (kosť)  
- Ų / Ѫ → (bųdų / бѫдѫ)  
- Ź → (knęź / кнѧзь)

## 3. All ligatures (e.g. iotated yus) are removed due to the rule: > 1 Cyrillic letter = 1 Latin letter

# DECISIONS FROM 2017-06-26

1. Writing consistency: in one text or „koň, koňa” or „konj, konja”. _(this one comes from the problem we had with huge variety of Slavic alphabets; so a good practice was to use one, consistent ortography in a given text in order to avoid confusion by a unprepared reader – annotation from 28.04.2026, M.S.)_
2. In simple ortography the prefixes are „so-, voz-, vo-”, in the expanded one „sò-, vòz-, vò-”
3. Forms were introduced: pekti, mogti
ja pekų / mogų
pečem / možem etc.
4. we retained the form "nema(je)"  _(in the meaning of „there isn't/aren't” – annotation from 28.04.2026, M.S.)_
5. Restricted use of instrumental in situations like „Moj pes jest kot', not „Moj pes jest kotom”  _(it's a general recommendation without further details – annotation from 28.04.2026, M.S.)_
6. Two equal forms of the verb „to have” are allowed" iměti = imati (imějų / imajų / imam / iměm) ⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️  _(right now the dictionary doesn't show this difference – annotation from 28.04.2026, M.S.)_
7. Forms were introduced: dělajųći, govoręći, dělavši, govorivši etc.
8. "sedm, osm, jesm" instead of "sedem, osem, jesem"
9. the suffix „-nadsęť” was introduced: 12, 13 - dvanadsęť, trinadsęť…
10. the basic form of numerals 200-900 is „dvasto-devęťsto” but using „dvěstě-devęťsòt” forms is also a possibility
11. writing of foreign Byzantine words like e.g.„historija” is with „h-”, and not ”istorija”
12. the form „tysęć” was introduced
13. Two patterns of numerals 2-4 declension were introduced: GEN: dvoh, trěh, četyrěh
DAT: dvom, trěm, četyrěm
INS: dvoma, trěmi, četyrěmi
LOC: dvoh, trěh, četyrěh
14. The inflection of the word „veś” is same as in the word „moj” with the disappearing ”e”
15. The inflection pattern of the word „sej” was removed, the word „sej” is retained as archai and inflected like „moj”
16. the discussion about writing  „jih, jim” and „prijateli” continued, no clear decisions were made.
17. The verbs with the infinitive ending „-iti!” have always the imperative with „-i!” (govori!, hti!, koristi! lųči!), the forms with the imperative ending as „govorj! koristj! lųč!” are removed. ⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️  _(unclear as as the given example of „hti!” belongs to „htěti” which has the „-ěti” edning; meanwhile in the dictionary we can see forms like „vladěj!” – annotation from 28.04.2026, M.S.)_

# DECISIONS FROM 2017-06-19

1. the basic form of the feminine vocative pattern: „-e!”(duše! zemje!) due to Old Church Slavonic.  The ending „-o!” is an additional possibility (dušo! zemjo!) ⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️  
2. at the verbs having the ending „-iti” the suffix „-ęt” was introduced: „oni govoręt, vidęt”.  As an additional possibility there is the ending „-'ųt”: „oni govorjųt, vidžųt”. Because of that forms like „govoręći, vidęći” are the standard and „govorjųći, vidžųći” are additional and not mandatory. ⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️  
3. the forms „ja vidžų, prošų, nesų, dělam” are maintained, additional forms are „ja viděm, prosim, nesem, dělajų”. If I understood correctly, the forms with „-aje” remain as additional. ⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️
4. Jan said that the results of the discussions on the Facebook and the mail about the ky/ki writing, if I remember correctly, will be prepared by him on a table. ⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️   _(here an attachment should be provided – annotation from 28.04.2026, M.S.)_
