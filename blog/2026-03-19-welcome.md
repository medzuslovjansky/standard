Welcome to **interslavic.online** — a new home for the Interslavic language on the web.

<!-- truncate -->

This site is in **alpha**. We are compiling and organizing existing sources — with humans and AI working together — to build a comprehensive resource for the Interslavic language community.

## What's here now

- **The Standard** — a pre-alpha specification of the Interslavic language, being assembled from existing materials and community knowledge
- **Governance** — information about the Committee and Foundation
- **Community** — events, media coverage, and partner organizations

## What's coming

Many sections are stubs with outlines, waiting to be filled in. This is intentional — we've mapped the territory before filling it in, so contributors can see where help is needed.

Extension points and gaps are clearly marked. If you'd like to contribute, visit the [Develop](/develop) page or join us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/interslavic).

## A note on the standard

The language specification at `/standard/` is **pre-alpha**. It is being assembled from existing grammar descriptions, academic work, and community consensus. It does not yet represent the official position of the Committee of the Interslavic Language — that process is ongoing.

Work is active. Watch this space.
