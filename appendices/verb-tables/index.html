Unverified Content

This table has structural issues: the header declares 4 columns but rows contain 5 cells (two unlabeled present-tense columns). Additionally, the simple past forms appear incorrect for a vowel stem. Special past passive rules:

  • -iti verbs: -jeny (not -iny): ljubiti→ljubjeny, prositi→prošeny
  • -nuti verbs: -neny: tegnuti→tegneny
  • Monosyllabic stems (except -a): -ty: biti→bity, čuti→čuty

Verbal nouns (gerunds)​

Replace -ny → -nje or -ty → -tje: dělanje "doing", nesenje "carrying", pitje "drinking"

Verbs of Motion (three-way aspect)​

Unverified Content

The source discusses the three-way aspect pattern for verbs of motion but only gives hoditi/idti/pojdti and jezditi/jehati as explicit examples. The full table with letati/letěti, nositi/nesti, voziti/vezti, běgati/běgti, and plovati/pluti is not present in the source material and appears to be extrapolated.

Non-directional impf.Directional impf.Perfective
hoditi "walk (around)"idti "go (toward)"pojdti "set off"
jezditi "ride (around)"jehati "ride (toward)"pojehati "set off by vehicle"
letati "fly (around)"letěti "fly (toward)"poletěti "fly off"
